============
Appointments
============

The **Appointments** app is a self-service scheduling tool that lets clients and prospects book
meetings, consultations, or services directly from an online booking page. Integrated with **CRM**,
**Calendar**, and other Odoo apps, it allows companies to automate appointment scheduling based on
the real-time availability of users' calendars or shared resources, such as meeting rooms or
equipment.
