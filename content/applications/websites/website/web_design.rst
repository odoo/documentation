==========
Web design
==========

Design your website using :doc:`Building blocks <../website/web_design/building_blocks>`, shape your
:doc:`Themes <../website/web_design/themes>` with various options, structure and present content with
:doc:`Elements <../website/web_design/elements>` and display or hide building blocks using
:doc:`Visibility <../website/web_design/visibility>` options.

.. cards::

   .. card:: Building blocks
      :target: web_design/building_blocks
      :large:

      Design your website by dragging and dropping building blocks, then editing them to fit your
      content and layout needs.

   .. card:: General theme
      :target: web_design/themes
      :large:

      Shape your website’s theme with various options for its colors, fonts, and layout.

   .. card:: Elements
      :target: web_design/elements
      :large:

      Structure and present content effectively with elements such as titles, lists, etc.

   .. card:: Visibility
      :target: web_design/visibility
      :large:

      Display or hide building blocks based on several criteria.
