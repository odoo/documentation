==================================
Odoo electronic invoicing in Egypt
==================================

Odoo Invoicing is your trusted partner for safe, efficient, and legally compliant e-invoicing
solutions tailored to meet Egypt’s regulatory standards and compatible with the requirements set
forth by the Egyptian Tax Authority.

Legal framework for e-invoicing in Egypt
========================================

In Egypt, businesses must comply with electronic invoicing regulations established by the `Egyptian
Tax Authority (ETA) <https://www.eta.gov.eg/sites/default/files/2023-03/digital-service-guide-english.pdf>`_
as part of the country’s tax `digitalization <https://invoicing.eta.gov.eg/content>`_ initiative.
The system requires taxpayers within the mandated phases to electronically issue and submit
invoices, credit notes, and debit notes through the ETA platform, using the prescribed JSON
structure and validation requirements. Documents submitted to the ETA are validated and assigned a
unique UUID to ensure traceability and compliance. Depending on the taxpayer category and
transaction type, businesses are required to use digital signatures and standardized product coding
systems such as EGS or GS1 codes. The scope of mandatory adoption continues to expand as Egypt
advances its tax compliance and digital transformation objectives.

Compliance with Egypt’s e-invoicing regulations
===============================================

Odoo invoicing simplifies the process of adhering to Egypt’s e-invoicing regulations, offering
features designed to ensure compliance:

- **Supported formats**: Odoo supports the ETA-required JSON formats for electronic invoices, credit
  notes, and debit notes. This ensures documents comply with Egyptian e-invoicing technical
  requirements, including mandatory invoice fields, tax details, discounts, and document references.
- **ETA integration**: Odoo integrates with the ETA’s APIs to automate document submission,
  validation, UUID synchronization, and status tracking directly from the ERP system. Odoo also
  supports document workflow management, including handling rejected documents according to ETA
  rules.
- **Secure storage and retrieval**: Odoo securely stores electronic documents and ETA responses
  within the system, allowing businesses to retrieve submitted invoices.
- **Automatic VAT calculation and reporting**: Odoo Invoicing helps businesses comply with Egyptian
  VAT requirements by automatically calculating taxes, applying compliant tax structures, and
  ensuring invoices follow the format and validation rules defined by the ETA for electronic
  invoicing transactions.
