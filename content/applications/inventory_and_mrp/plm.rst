============================
Product lifecycle management
============================

Odoo's *Product Lifecycle Management*, or *PLM* offers a systematic approach for testing,
collaboration, and iteration of products across concept development, design, manufacturing,
marketing, and post-launch support stages.

With approvals, key stakeholders can review changes before implementation.

Install PLM
===========

Despite being used to iterate on **Manufacturing** bills of materials, **PLM** is *not* installed by
default when installing the **Manufacturing** app. It must be installed separately to take advantage
of its benefits.

To install **PLM**, open the **Apps** app. Search for `PLM`, then click :guilabel:`Install` on the
:guilabel:`Product Lifecycle Management (PLM)` card.

.. note::
   Adding or removing apps can affect other apps and modify subscription costs. See
   :doc:`../general/apps_modules` to learn more.
