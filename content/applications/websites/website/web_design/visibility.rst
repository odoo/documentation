==========
Visibility
==========

You can choose to display or hide building blocks based on a visitor's:

- device
- country
- website language
- UTM parameters

To toggle the visibility of a building block based on the visitor's device type, open the website
editor, and select a block or a column element within a block. In the :guilabel:`Customize` tab,
scroll down to :guilabel:`Visibility` under the block or column customization options, and click the
|mobile icon| (:guilabel:`Show/Hide on Mobile`) or |desktop icon| (:guilabel:`Show/Hide on Desktop`)
button. Click on :guilabel:`Save` to apply the changes to your website.

Conditions
==========

To access the language, country, and :doc:`UTM <../reporting/link_tracker>` conditions, open the
website editor and select a building block. In the :guilabel:`Customize` tab, scroll down to
:guilabel:`Visibility`, click :guilabel:`No condition`, and select :guilabel:`Conditionally` instead
to display them.

- :guilabel:`Country`: the country of the visitor's IP address.
- :guilabel:`Languages`: the website language the visitor selected. This option is only available if
  your website is translated into at least one language besides the original.
- :guilabel:`UTM Campaign`
- :guilabel:`UTM Medium`
- :guilabel:`UTM Source`
- :guilabel:`Users`: based on whether visitors are logged in or everyone.

Click on :guilabel:`Save` to apply the changes to your website.

Invisible elements
==================

Hidden blocks and elements are listed at the bottom of the :guilabel:`Blocks`, :guilabel:`Customize`
, and :guilabel:`Edit` menus. Select one to display it in the website editor. This allows you to see
the elements you have made invisible while working in the website editor, but it does not change
their visibility status on your website's front-end. Click on :guilabel:`Save` to apply the changes
to your website.
