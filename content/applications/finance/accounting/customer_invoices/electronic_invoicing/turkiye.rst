====================================
Odoo electronic invoicing in Türkiye
====================================

Odoo Invoicing is your trusted partner for safe, efficient, and legally compliant e-invoicing
solutions tailored to meet Türkiye’s regulatory standards and compatible with the requirements set
forth by the Turkish Revenue Administration.

Legal framework for e-invoicing in Türkiye
==========================================

In Türkiye, businesses must comply with electronic invoicing regulations established by the Turkish
Revenue Administration (Gelir İdaresi Başkanlığı – GİB) under the `Tax Procedure Law No. 213 (VUK) <https://www.gib.gov.tr/mevzuat/kanun/434>`_
and the `General Communiqué No. 509 <https://www.resmigazete.gov.tr/eskiler/2019/10/20191019-5.pdf>`_.
These regulations govern the issuance, transmission, and storage of electronic invoices through the
centralized GİB platform using the UBL-TR XML standard. Businesses subject to mandatory e-invoicing
must use the e-Fatura system for B2B and B2G transactions, while e-Arşiv Fatura is used for
transactions involving non-registered recipients, including many B2C sales. Mandatory adoption is
based on turnover thresholds and sector-specific requirements, and the scope continues to expand to
improve tax compliance, transparency, and digitalization across Türkiye’s tax system.

Compliance with Türkiye’s e-invoicing regulations
=================================================

Odoo invoicing simplifies the process of adhering to Türkiye’s e-invoicing regulations, offering
features designed to ensure compliance:

- **Supported formats**: Odoo supports the standardized UBL-TR 1.2.1 XML format required by the
  Turkish Revenue Administration (GİB) for e-Fatura and e-Arşiv invoices. This ensures invoices
  comply with Turkish e-invoicing regulations for both B2B and B2C transactions. Odoo also supports
  integration with certified GİB private integrator for compliant invoice transmission.
- **Secure storage and retrieval**: Odoo sends electronic invoice XML files to Nilvera, a certified
  private integrator, which securely stores the data on servers located in Türkiye in accordance
  with Turkish e-invoicing and data retention requirements. This ensures invoices remain accessible
  for retrieval, audits, and legal compliance purposes.
- **Automatic VAT calculation and reporting**: Odoo Invoicing helps businesses comply with Turkish
  VAT requirements by automatically calculating and including VAT in invoices and ensuring that each
  invoice follows the legal format defined by GİB for electronic invoicing transactions.
