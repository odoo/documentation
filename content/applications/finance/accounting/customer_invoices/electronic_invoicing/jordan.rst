===================================
Odoo electronic invoicing in Jordan
===================================

Odoo Invoicing is your trusted partner for safe, efficient, and legally compliant e-invoicing
solutions tailored to meet Jordan’s regulatory standards and compatible with the requirements set
forth by the Income and Sales Tax Department.

Legal framework for e-invoicing in Jordan
=========================================

In Jordan, businesses must comply with `electronic invoicing regulations <https://istd.gov.jo/EN/List/Electronic_billing_User_Manual>`_
established by the `Income and Sales Tax Department (ISTD) <https://istd.gov.jo/En/List/Laws>`_
through the Jordanian National Electronic Invoicing System, commonly referred to as JoFotara. The
system requires businesses within the mandated scope to issue and submit electronic invoices and
related debit and credit notes through the JoFotara platform in accordance with the government’s
technical and legal requirements. Businesses must generate invoices in the required UBL 2.1 format
and submit them for real-time validation through the JoFotara platform. Upon successful validation,
invoices receive a QR code used for verification and compliance purposes. Depending on the taxpayer
type and applicable tax regime, invoices may require percentage-based taxes, fixed taxes, or both.
The framework aims to improve tax compliance, transparency, and digitalization across Jordan’s tax
system.

Compliance with Jordan’s e-invoicing regulations
================================================

Odoo invoicing simplifies the process of adhering to Jordan’s e-invoicing regulations, offering
features designed to ensure compliance:

- **Supported formats**: Odoo supports the standardized UBL 2.1 electronic invoice format required
  by JoFotara for invoices, debit notes, and credit notes.
- **JoFotara integration**: Odoo integrates directly with the Jordanian National Electronic
  Invoicing System (JoFotara) to automate invoice submission, validation, QR code synchronization,
  and invoice status tracking directly from the system. Odoo also supports real-time validation
  workflows for invoices and related debit and credit notes.
- **Secure storage and retrieval**: Odoo securely stores electronic invoices, QR codes, and JoFotara
  responses within the system, allowing businesses to retrieve compliant records for audits,
  operational purposes, and legal compliance requirements.
- **Automatic VAT calculation and reporting**: Odoo Invoicing helps businesses comply with Jordanian
  VAT requirements by automatically calculating taxes, applying compliant invoice structures, and
  ensuring invoices follow the technical and fiscal rules defined by the ISTD and JoFotara for
  electronic invoicing transactions.
